* Data may be deleted if (1) they correspond to duplicate entries or (2) errors. Publication of the same set of samples in different papers by the same authors is a serious problem that has led to duplicates entries in the database in the past. We now perform systematic searches of the database under the authorâ€™s name to identify earlier entries that may correspond to the same dataset. We have also extensively reviewed the entire dataset in order to find and eliminate these duplicates. However, despite these efforts, some duplicates may remain in the database and their identification is an ongoing task.
