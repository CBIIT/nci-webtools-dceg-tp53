Germline Variants

Explore data for individuals that are carriers of a TP53 germline variant and families in which at least one family member has been identified as a carrier of a germline variant in the TP53 gene.

Search Germline Variant Data
This option allows the retrieval and analysis of TP53 variants reported as germline events in human individuals. Each dataset entry corresponds to a tumor identified in an individual carrier of a TP53 germline variation. The searchable dataset only includes cancer-affected individuals who are confirmed or obligatory carrier of a TP53 mutation (data on non-affected carriers or non-confirmed carrier can be retrieved by downloading the full dataset with the 'data downloads' option).
