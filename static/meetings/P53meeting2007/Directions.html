  • LYON - SAINT EXUPÉRY airport:

    There is a shuttle bus to and from the airport (IARC is close to Grange-Blanche stop), Satobus: http://www.satobus.com

    Timetables: http://www.satobus.com/eng/horaire.html (Please foresee that the drivers may adapt their route according to the traffic in Lyon).

    Fares: http://www.satobus.com/eng/tarif_gb_04.html

    By taxi: fares are about 35-40 euros (but up to 65 euros at night).


  • Geneva international airport:

    Shuttle Service Lyon-Geneva: http://www.gva.ch/Desktopdefault.aspx/tabid-69/




  • PART DIEU station:

    To IARC: take metro B direction Gerland, change at Saxe-Gambetta and take metro D direction Venissieux-Grange blanche, get off at Grange-Blanche.

    From IARC: take metro D direction Vaise, change at Saxe-Gambetta and take metro B direction Charpennes-Part dieu, get off at Part dieu.

  • PERRACHE station:

    To IARC: take metro A direction Laurent-Bonnevay/Hotel de ville, change at Bellecour and take metro D direction Venissieux-Grange blanche, get off at Grange-Blanche.

    From IARC: take metro D direction Vaise, change at Bellecour and take metro A direction Perrache, get off at Perrache.
